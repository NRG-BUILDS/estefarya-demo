How far we've come

At Estefarya, we believe confidence begins with care, and every change should feel personal. Our name reflects that belief: “Este” for aesthetics, and “Farya,” meaning fairy or princess, a nod to transformation that feels graceful, not clinical. Founded in Turkey and trusted across borders, Estefarya stands apart as a certified medical clinic not an agent or broker. We offer expert treatments in hair restoration, plastic surgery, and cosmetic dentistry, with complete support that includes aftercare, accommodation, and translation.
